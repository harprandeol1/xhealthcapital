We back founders and established operators building scalable medical practices, wellness platforms, and healthcare technology. No generic funds—every dollar we deploy is inside the healthcare ecosystem.

What we do

X Health Capital invests in corporations and scalable healthcare businesses—from single-site clinics with a clear roll-out plan to multi-location platforms and healthcare software.

We also structure crowdfunding campaigns and plug founders into a curated network of angel investors who understand health services, reimbursement, and healthcare compliance.

How we invest

We only invest in healthcare projects

Our mandate is narrow by design: we invest in healthcare, and nothing else. That focus lets us move quickly on diligence, underwriting, and post-investment support.

What we look for

We back founders and established operators who are building scalable healthcare businesses with a clear path to multi-site expansion or technology-enabled growth.

  • Licensed medical practices and healthcare corporations.
  • Roll-out plans for additional locations or services.
  • Professionalized operations, clean financials, and compliance mindset.
  • Founders who want a long-term partner, not just a check.
How capital is sourced

Depending on stage and structure, we:

  • Invest directly from our own balance sheet and vehicles.
  • Structure crowdfunding campaigns for community-backed healthcare projects.
  • Connect you with angel investors who specialize in health services and health tech.

We only invest in corporations and scalable business models—no one-off lifestyle practices without a growth plan.
