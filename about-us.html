About Us

Why We Only Invest in Healthcare

xHealth Capital was founded on a simple thesis: the strongest EB-5 investments are the ones tied to services communities cannot go without. That's why every project we sponsor, without exception, is a licensed healthcare facility.

Our focus

We operate as a regional center and fund manager exclusively within eight healthcare verticals: primary care clinics, urgent care centers, specialist medical offices, hospice and palliative care, outpatient surgery centers, diagnostic imaging centers, skilled nursing facilities, and senior/assisted living communities. We do not sponsor hotels, multifamily housing, or other real estate categories common elsewhere in the EB-5 industry. That focus lets our underwriting team build deep expertise in healthcare licensing, reimbursement dynamics, staffing ratios, and operator selection — the specific factors that determine whether a healthcare project will hit its job-creation numbers and remain financially sound through the I-829 stage and beyond.

How we select projects

  1. Licensed, experienced operators only. We partner with healthcare operators who already have a track record in their specific care category — we don't fund first-time operators with unproven clinical or business models.
  2. Independent job-creation analysis. Every project's job-creation methodology is prepared by a qualified, independent economist using accepted USCIS-recognized input-output models.
  3. TEA verification. We confirm Targeted Employment Area status with current census and labor data before offering a project at the reduced $800,000 threshold.
  4. Full financial transparency. Every offering memorandum includes underlying project financials, construction/operating milestones, and risk factors for investor review.

Who we serve

We work with investors at every stage of their U.S. journey — from F1 students and H1B visa holders already living in the U.S., to families investing from India, the UAE, and around the world. Regardless of where you're starting from, our team provides the same free eligibility review and access to full offering documentation.
