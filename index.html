Investment Criteria

What we look for in a healthcare project

We're selective and move fast when the right project comes along. Here's what we focus on.

✓

Outpatient or Clinic-Based Model

We focus on outpatient healthcare — urgent care, primary care, imaging, ASC, therapy, and wellness. Hospital or inpatient models are outside our scope.

✓

Experienced Operator or Clinical Partner

We invest alongside founders who have hands-on clinical, operational, or healthcare business experience. Credential matters.

✓

Clear Revenue Model

Insurance reimbursement, membership, cash-pay, or hybrid — we need to understand the payer mix and revenue logic before moving forward.

✓

U.S.-Based Operations

All projects must be based in the United States. EB-5 eligible projects must meet USCIS job creation requirements.

✓

De Novo or Acquisition

We fund both new clinic launches and acquisitions of existing healthcare businesses — including multi-site platform builds.

✓

Capital Use Transparency

We expect founders to show how capital is deployed — equipment, buildout, staffing, working capital — with clear timelines and milestones.
