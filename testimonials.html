Real stories from investors who chose xHealth Capital's healthcare-only approach for their family's Green Card journey.

"The rural TEA urgent care project let us qualify at the lower investment threshold while supporting a community that genuinely needed the facility."

"Knowing our capital was funding an outpatient surgery center — not speculative real estate — made the EB-5 decision much easier for our family."
